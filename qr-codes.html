Scan to Order — Table QR Codes

Instructions for staff: Open this page in any browser, then use File → Print (or Ctrl+P / ⌘P) to print all 8 QR codes. Cut each card and place it on its respective table or counter. Customers scan the QR code to open the menu — their table number is automatically included in every order sent to WhatsApp.
